About me

SoyFire

Hello! My name is SoyFire, I am a spanish Blockbench Artist, I have been making, animating and texturing models for more than 3 years now. I specialize in animating 3D models and I can also make both models and textures (including animated ones) to be used in the game of Minecraft. I have advanced knowledge about the Java edition of the game, and a very basic knowledge of the Bedrock edition. Scroll down to see some of my best work. I hope you enjoy it!
